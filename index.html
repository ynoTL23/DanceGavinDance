About

Assembling in 2005, Dance Gavin Dance is a post-hardcore rock band from Sacramento, California with many successful albums. Currently, the band's line up showcases lead singer Tilian Pearson, screamer Jon Mess, lead guitarist Will Swan, drummer Matt Mingus, and Tim Feerick on the bass guitar.
